When clients ask...

  • Are you available for new projects?

    Don't just say yes or no — tell them which days of the week you can commit to a new project and when you can start.

  • What will you deliver?

    Agree to the deliverables for each project along with estimates to set clear expectations of when each will be completed.

  • Have you made any progress?

    Send daily and weekly status emails with a description of what you worked on and where the overall project stands.

When you wonder...

  • What am I working on today?

    See what you're working on each day of the week, which tasks to focus on first, and who you're working with to get them done.

  • How much should I charge?

    Know how your rate compares to others so you can confidently say to prospective clients that yes, your work is worth it.

  • How and when do I pay my estimated taxes?

    After completing a project you'll see exactly how much to put aside for taxes and when your quarterly payments are due.
